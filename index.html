How We Worked This Month

March content was built around two key themes: summer heat awareness and brand trust. As temperatures begin to rise, we positioned Dhakshan Cool Roof Tiles as the most relevant solution for homeowners worried about roof heat. Content like "Is your house too hot?" and "Don't turn your roof into an oven" connected directly with the pain points of the target audience.

Relatable formats such as the owner-versus-employee meme and the employee fun video generated the highest engagement of the month, proving that entertaining content builds the brand affinity that converts audiences into customers.
💡 Facebook gained 100 new followers this month through entirely organic posting — no paid promotion. The "Appreciate the team" post alone drove 31 new followers and 546 views, showing that people-first content builds the deepest audience connection.

Overall Performance Analysis

Instagram recorded 10,385 total views across 19 posts. The March 6 trending reel became the month's top Instagram post with 4,055 views and 44 likes, driven by the relatable owner-versus-employee format. The fun video on March 15 generated the highest likes count of the month at 57 and sparked 7 comments, showing the audience actively engaging with entertaining content.

YouTube maintained strong daily viewership with 9,495 total views across 11 videos. The Women's Day tribute video was the top performer at 1,694 views with 29 likes and 4 new subscribers in a single upload. The channel gained 14 new subscribers organically across the month, reflecting growing viewer loyalty.

Facebook was the standout follower growth platform in March, adding 100 new followers entirely through organic content. Four posts contributed more than 10 new followers each, led by the Employees Intro post which brought in 31 followers from a single day. This confirms that team-focused and people-centred content resonates most strongly with the Facebook audience.
